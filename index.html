About Me

I was born in Southern California and grew up on the East Coast. I live in Los Angeles where I work, study, commute and raise a family with my wife and son. Currently learning Java and Dad Skills.

I graduated from Naropa University with a Bachelors in English and worked at a few different jobs before getting into startups, computer science and awesome pourover coffee. The shift from Creative Writing to Computer Science gives me the creativity of content creation and and adds the interaction between user and computer program. Due to this interactivity and multimedia approach I am hooked. There are new technologies and ideas cropping up every day and it is an exciting field to be in. It all started with a gray Macintosh IIgs when I was a kid. I spent as much time as I could trying different programs and messing with macPaint. I have been interested in computers ever since. Of course I have now spent time using PC, Linux, VR etc. This website is for sharing some small projects I'm working on, making posts and to consolidate my Github, LinkedIn and other various account together in one place.
